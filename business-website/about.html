Our story

Rooted in the seasons.

Urban Garden Bistro was born from a simple idea: bring the warmth of a neighborhood garden and the excitement of city dining together on one plate.

From market stall to bistro

What started as a weekend market stall quickly grew into a full-service restaurant. Our founders, chef Lina and beverage director Marco, shared a love for seasonal produce and honest, ingredient-first cooking.

Today, we work directly with small farms and makers to create a menu that changes with the weather, the harvest, and our curiosity.

What we believe

  • Food tastes better when you know where it comes from.
  • Dining out should feel welcoming, never intimidating.
  • Small details make a big difference.
